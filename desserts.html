ADVOCACY IN ACADEMIA
I share some of my lowest and highest points of grad school on the StoryCollider podcast (starting 14'45"). Experiences like these led me to advocate for culture change in academia. At Harvard, I was a leader of SEAS InTouch, a peer advising group supporting engineering graduate students. I'm a graduate of the Harvard Peer Coaching Initiative and have also collaborated with the Harvard Academic Resource Center to create programming to address the ups and downs of graduate life.


CLIMBING
Since 2018, I've been active in the Harvard Mountaineering Club. I served on the DEI committee and as a board member and built initiatives and processes to make climbing more accessible to all.

I am a member of Alpine Ascents International's 2023-24 BIPOC Guide Development Program. As part of this program, I'm deepening my technical skills on a diverse range of terrain -- rock, alpine, snow, glacier -- and learning how to instruct, guide, and empower clients in building their own relationships to the mountains.
