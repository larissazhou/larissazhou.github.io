WELCOME TO THE CIRCUS
The circus is for the misfits, the bold, the brave, and the wildly curious.

Being in the circus isn’t for everyone. Most people are content to sit in the audience. For some, though, there’s no other place they’d rather be. They can’t imagine life without the adventure, the curiosity, the audacious leaps into the unknown.

If you’re here, it’s because you belong in the circus. You embrace the thrill of the tightrope and the challenge of juggling a million ideas at once. But life in the circus isn’t always easy:

• The rules of the “real world” weigh you down with ideas about what’s good, correct, or possible.
• Your boldness and curiosity often come with self-doubt, hesitation, or the feeling of being misunderstood.
• Extraordinary achievement seems to require extraodinary suffering. Sometimes, you wonder if being just a little more “normal” would bring more contentment—and whether the trade-off is worth it.

WORK WITH ME
You don’t have to choose between bold ambition and contentment.

How do I know this is possible? Because I'm doing the work in my own life.

Together, we can untangle the stories and expectations that are weighing you down—the ones that tell you success must be hard, relentless, and lonely.

When we work together, we’ll focus on:
• Unpacking the angst: Understanding why certain patterns keep showing up and how to move through them.
• Making bold choices with ease: Finding the intersection of your ambition and values so decisions feel clear and energizing.
• Creating extraordinary results without extraordinary suffering: Building systems and practices that sustain your creativity, curiosity, and joy—so the circus doesn’t feel like a grind.
